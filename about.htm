About Me

Biography

After graduating cum laude from New York University's Music Business program, Brenda transitioned from intern to Assistant Programming Manager within the AOL Programming division based in New York City. As part of the product development team for the Entertainment websites, including AOL Music, Spinner, AOL Moviefone, AOL Television, she focused on optimizing content, search integrations, and the overall user experience through use of web analytics.

She later moved back to her hometown, Chicago, where she currently works at a brand marketing design agency located in the north suburbs.
